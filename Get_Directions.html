Get Directions

Driving Directions from Fort Smith:

-Take I-40 East to exit 58 (Rogers Road).

-Proceed North for two miles. At the stop light, turn left on Main Street.

-Proceed on Main Street for one half mile, at stop light turn right on College Avenue.

-Stay on College Avenue for three quarters mile, entering the University of the Ozarks campus.

-The University Mall (academic buildings) will be on your left.

-On your right will be MacLean Residents Hall and a large group of tennis courts. Immediately after the tennis courts is a parking lot - PARK THERE!

-Once parked, proceed north along College Ave. You will see Bagwell-Jones Residence Hall on the right and next to it is the Cary-Wortz Residents Hall where Greystone is located.
