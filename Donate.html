Donate to Greystone

Greystone is a 501(c)(3) not-for-profit organization and as such, any donations or contributions provided to Greystone can be utilized as a tax deduction. If you are interested in supporting Greystone, your contribution can be made directly to Greystone by mailing your check to:

Greystone Preparatory School

415 North College Avenue

Clarksville, AR  72830 

Additionally, you can make a contribution to Greystone online via our PayPal account by clicking the Donation button below.

Regardless of the amount of your contribution or donation, 100% of your gift will be applied directly to the General Dave Palmer Greystone Scholarship Fund - Greystone will preserve your entire gift for our students. Award of Greystone scholarship money is based upon the financial assessment provided by the family to ensure those most in need receive priority assistance.
