What’s in a name?

Our students many challenges as they pursue their nominations and academy appointments are often compared to the efforts put forth when climbing a mountain. In the early days of the program development, I read some of Sir Edmond Hillary's memoirs. As a world-renowned mountain climber and the first person to summit Mount Everest, he was all too familiar with extreme challenges. I discovered that he kept referring to "grey stone" and how he would look for it whenever he would climb. According to Hillary, this "grey stone" was granitic and therefore strong enough to hold a climber's weight. Finding the "grey stone" was imperative because he knew he could depend on it, and it would not let him down – literally! For some reason, that story stuck with me. As the program's development continued, we began to consider names, and Greystone came to mind!

The challenge before you as an academy candidate is often daunting, and at times, it seems impossible, but for those willing to fully engage this challenge and persevere, the reward at the summit is worth all the struggles encountered during the ascent. As the Greystone program's Founder, I intended to create a program that would be both meaningful and yet applicable to the many challenges faced by candidates. How could I use the potential and power of existing programs within college-level institutions to create an academic, athletic, and leadership environment above and beyond the existing academy preparatory achievement level? It took six years to develop the baseline program for Greystone. Over the last 17 years of operation, the program continues to grow and evolve. The engine behind this change has been our students – and in particular, our Greystone graduates. Their feedback based upon their recent academy experience and the admission's guidance provided by the academies ensures that Greystone remains on the cutting edge of academy preparatory education excellence. Every year as our students graduate from Greystone and enter the academies; they provide us with suggestions to improve the program – taking our program to the next higher level. Due to our relationship with the University of the Ozarks, we incorporated all Greystone program changes. So long as there are candidates interested in reapplying to the academies, we will continue delivering the best possible academy preparatory experience! If you are not willing to give up on your dream of serving our nation as an academy trained and educated leader, trust in Greystone and invest in this highly successful program…we won't let you down!
